About Us

We are a non-profit organization dedicated to rescuing and rehoming dogs in need. Our mission is to provide a safe haven for homeless and abandoned dogs while working tirelessly to find them loving forever homes.

Our team of dedicated volunteers ensures that each dog receives the care and love they deserve.

Indian Breeds

Bakharwal

The Bakharwal dog is a livestock guardian dog found in northern India. It is an ancient working Indian dog breed found in Ladakh and across the Pir Panjal Range of Jammu and Kashmir, India.

View more
Jonangi

The Jonangi is an Indian dog breed, mostly found in Andhra Pradesh and along the east coast from West Bengal to Tamil Nadu. It was once found in and around Kolleru Lake in West Godavari.

View more
Mudhol Hound

The Mudhol Hound, also known as the Caravan Hound, is a breed of sighthound from India. The Kennel Club of India (KCI) and Indian National Kennel Club recognize the breed under different breed names.
